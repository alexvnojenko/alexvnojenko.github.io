Many people naturally have a loud voice, or who have a particular speaking style many come across to some as being arrogant or insecure. How can we identify with general accuracy when someone is insecure, arrogant, or toxic without making a false assumption on their personality? Obviously, foul language and disrespectful behaviour can never be attributed to personality and is always a flaw. Likewise, verbal abuse and screaming isn't a "loud voice" but most definitely a flaw. Manipulating others and not being able to handle internal pressure *is* a flaw and NOT related to personality.
